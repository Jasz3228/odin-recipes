Bacon Cheeseburger

The following will prepare you to make Bacon cheeseburgers

Ingredients

  • 1lb slice bacon
  • 5lbs ground beef
  • 1 large onion
  • 1/4 cup steak sauce
  • salt and pepper
  • 1lb American cheese slices

Steps

  1. Preheat grill to high
  2. Cook bacon crispy
  3. Place patties on grill 5min per side
  4. Add cheese last minute of cooking
